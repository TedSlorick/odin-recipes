Ted's Chicken Stir Fry

Return Home!

Description

A quick and comforting weekday night meal using chicken thighs, broccoli, peppers. Make this meal in just 25 minutes and keep everyone in the family happy. Best served with rice.

Ingredients

Stir Fry

  • 4 Large Chicken Thighs 1 inch cubes
  • 1 Red Pepper sliced
  • 1 Head Broccoli chopped
  • MSG

Sauce

  • 1 cup Soy Sauce
  • 2 Tbsp Toasted Sesame Oil
  • 2 Tbsp Honey
  • 1 Tsp Chilli Flakes
  • 4 Cloves of Garlic fine diced
  • Thumb Sized piece of ginger fine diced
  • Splash of water
  • Teaspoon of cornstarch

To Garnish

  • Green Onion Sliced

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a large frying pan, once at a medium-high temperature add the chicken and cook for 5-8 minutes or until just cooked through.
  2. Whilst the chicken is cooking prepare the other vegetables.
  3. Once cooked remove the chicken and set aside and add vegetables to frying pan.
  4. Put together sauce by combining all the sauce ingredients in a separate bowl and mixing thoroughly.
  5. Once vegetables are starting to soften add the chicken back in along with the sauce and turn heat to high.
  6. Cook al together for a few more minutes, until sauce has started to thicken and finish with some MSG for the extra addictiveness.
  7. Serve on rice and add the sliced green onion to garnish!
